What is Festival



A special day or period, usually in memory of a religious event, with its own social activities, food, or ceremonies

Religious Festival


A religious festival is a ceremonial event celebrated by adherents of a particular faith, often commemorating significant moments or figures in their religious tradition. These festivals typically involve rituals, prayers, feasting, and community gatherings, fostering spiritual connection and cultural identity among believers.
